1. What OptimalFit is

OptimalFit is a personal fitness-tracking tool. You log sleep, food, exercise, body metrics, water, and steps; the App analyzes that data on your device and shows trends, statistics, and suggestions (for example, which time of day your workouts tend to go best). An optional "Coach" feature can generate conversational answers about your data using a companion program that you run on your own computer.
