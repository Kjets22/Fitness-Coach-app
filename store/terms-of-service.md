## 1. What OptimalFit is

OptimalFit is a personal fitness-tracking tool. You log sleep, food, exercise, body metrics, water, and steps; the App analyzes that data **on your device** and shows trends, statistics, and suggestions. An optional "Coach" feature can generate conversational answers about your data using a companion program that you run on your own computer. An **optional community** lets you create an account, follow other users, and share posts you explicitly choose to publish — including "Receipt" stat cards, gym check-ins, and photos. Using the community is entirely optional; every tracking and coaching feature works without an account.
